ABOUT THE COMPANY

LOVE LETTER


Love Letter is a world wide organization that provides
the people with high quality stationery templates
which are fit for friends, family, and lovers.
The organization was formed in 2019 to bring
people closer together by giving them an easy access
of various letter templates which can be utilized
to send "love letters" to their loved ones.




ABOUT THE FOUNDER

KYLE CATIGNAS


Kyle Catignas is a well-known mechanical engineer
who earned a Bachelors Degree in BS MEEMTE
at De La Salle University - Manila Campus.
He created this organization as a side project
to show his appreciation for the hundreds of people
that were a part of his life such as his co-workers,
family members, and peers.
